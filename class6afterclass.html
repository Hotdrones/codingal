About The R.P.D

The Raccoon Police Department (R.P.D.) is the primary law enforcement agency in Raccoon City, a mid-sized American city located in the Midwestern United States. The R.P.D. is responsible for maintaining public order, enforcing laws, and providing emergency services to the residents of Raccoon City. The department is known for its specialized units, including the S.T.A.R.S. (Special Tactics and Rescue Service) team, which plays a significant role in combating bioterrorism threats within the city.

Requirments to join the R.P.D

  1. Must be at least 21 years old.
  2. Must have a valid driver's license.
  3. Must have a high school diploma or equivalent.
  4. Must pass a physical fitness test.
  5. Must pass a written exam.
  6. Must pass a background check.
  7. Must complete a police academy training program.
